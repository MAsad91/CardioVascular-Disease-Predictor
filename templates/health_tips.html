General Heart Health Tips

Physical Activity

Regular Exercise

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity per week.

Stay Active

Take regular breaks from sitting and incorporate movement into your daily routine.

Strength Training

Include muscle-strengthening activities at least twice a week for better heart health.

Consistency is Key

Maintain a regular exercise schedule and gradually increase intensity over time.

Nutrition

Heart-Healthy Diet

Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and limit sugary beverages and alcohol.

Portion Control

Practice mindful eating and maintain appropriate portion sizes for better heart health.

Limit Processed Foods

Reduce intake of processed foods, added sugars, and excessive salt in your diet.

Lifestyle

Quality Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support heart health.

Stress Management

Practice stress-reduction techniques like meditation, deep breathing, or yoga.

Quit Smoking

If you smoke, seek help to quit. Avoid secondhand smoke exposure.

Regular Check-ups

Schedule regular health screenings and maintain your medical appointments.

Preventive Care

Monitor Vital Signs

Regularly check your blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar levels.

Medication Adherence

Take prescribed medications as directed and maintain a medication schedule.

Social Support

Build a support network and stay connected with family and friends.

Health Education

Stay informed about heart health and learn to recognize warning signs.
