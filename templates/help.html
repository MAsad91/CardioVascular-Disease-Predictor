Getting Started

Welcome to the Heart Disease Prediction System! This AI-powered platform helps assess your cardiovascular risk using advanced machine learning algorithms. Here's how to get started:

Choose Your Assessment Method:

  1. 1Chat Assessment: Perfect for describing symptoms in natural language. Our AI will guide you through questions and provide personalized recommendations.
  2. 2Quick Assessment: Ideal for fast screening using 4 essential health metrics with smart defaults for accuracy.
  3. 3Detailed Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation using all 14 clinical parameters for the most accurate prediction.

Professional Cardiovascular Consultation

Our enhanced Chat Assessment now provides a professional, doctor-like consultation experience with comprehensive cardiovascular evaluation:

Consultation Process:

  1. Professional Greeting: Begin with Dr. Assistant who will guide you through a structured medical consultation
  2. Chief Complaint: Describe your main concern or reason for seeking assessment
  3. Detailed Symptom Analysis: Provide comprehensive details about any symptoms you're experiencing
  4. Medical History: Share your medical background, current medications, and existing conditions
  5. Risk Factor Assessment: Discuss lifestyle factors, family history, and other relevant information
  6. Professional Assessment: Receive a detailed clinical impression with specific recommendations

What Makes This Professional:

  • Medical Terminology: Uses appropriate clinical language and assessment methods
  • Structured Interview: Follows a systematic approach similar to real medical consultations
  • Emergency Detection: Immediate identification of potentially serious conditions
  • Comprehensive Questions: Explores symptom characteristics, duration, triggers, and patterns
  • Clinical Reasoning: Provides professional-level assessment and recommendations
  • Follow-up Guidance: Offers specific next steps based on your presentation
Important Disclaimer:

While this consultation provides professional-level guidance, it is for educational purposes only and does not replace actual medical care. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical diagnosis and treatment.

Understanding Your Results

Risk Levels:

  • Low Risk: Minimal cardiovascular concerns. Continue healthy lifestyle practices.
  • Medium Risk: Some risk factors present. Consider lifestyle modifications and regular monitoring.
  • High Risk: Significant risk factors identified. Recommend consultation with healthcare provider.

What Your Results Include:

  • Risk Probability: Percentage likelihood based on your health data
  • Key Risk Factors: Specific parameters contributing to your risk level
  • Personalized Recommendations: Tailored advice based on your profile
  • Next Steps: Actionable guidance for health improvement

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?
Our AI model achieves 95% accuracy using the K-Nearest Neighbors algorithm trained on the Cleveland Heart Disease dataset. However, this is a screening tool and should not replace professional medical diagnosis.
Is my health data secure?
Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All health information is processed locally and securely. No personal data is shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
Can I use this system multiple times?
Absolutely! You can perform multiple assessments to track changes in your health status over time. Use the "New Assessment" button to start fresh anytime.
What should I do with high-risk results?
High-risk results indicate you should consult with a healthcare professional promptly. The system provides specific recommendations and next steps to discuss with your doctor.
Do I need medical knowledge to use this system?
No medical expertise required! The Chat Assessment allows you to describe symptoms in plain language, while other assessments provide helpful explanations for all medical terms.

Technical Support

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Assessment not loading: Refresh the page and try again. Ensure JavaScript is enabled.
  • Results not displaying: Check your internet connection and try submitting again.
  • Form validation errors: Ensure all required fields are completed with valid values.
  • Chat not responding: Reload the page and restart the conversation.

System Requirements:

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • JavaScript enabled
  • Stable internet connection
  • Cookies enabled for session management

Emergency Information

This system is for screening and risk assessment only. If you're experiencing any of the following symptoms, seek immediate medical attention:

  • Severe chest pain or pressure
  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Pain radiating to arm, jaw, or back
  • Sudden severe headache
  • Loss of consciousness or severe dizziness
  • Rapid or irregular heartbeat with symptoms

Call emergency services (911) immediately if experiencing these symptoms.
